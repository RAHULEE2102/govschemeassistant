PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

PM Kisan gives eligible farmers ₹6,000 per year directly into their bank account in 3 instalments of ₹2,000 each. Simple guide to eligibility, documents, and how to apply.

Agriculture

⚠️ Independent Information Only

Simple Overview

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi is a central government scheme that gives small and marginal farmers ₹6,000 every year. The money is sent in three equal instalments of ₹2,000 each — directly into the farmer's bank account. This scheme started in February 2019 and aims to help farmers manage everyday expenses and reduce financial stress before and during the farming season.

Who Can Apply?

  • Farmers who own and cultivate agricultural land in their name
  • Small and marginal farming families (landholding up to 2 hectares)
  • Both men and women farmers are eligible
  • NOT eligible: Income tax payers, retired/serving government officers (Group A/B/C), professionals (doctors, lawyers, CAs), serving/former MPs/MLAs, institutional landholders

Income Limits

No fixed income limit. However, income tax payers and their family members are not eligible. The scheme is for small, low-income farming families.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • Land ownership record (Khasra-Khatoni / Jamabandi) in applicant's name
  • Bank account passbook (Aadhaar-linked)
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Aadhaar-linked mobile number

Step-by-Step Application Process

  1. 1
    Visit pmkisan.gov.in or nearest CSC

    Go to the official PM Kisan portal online, or visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) if you don't have internet access.

  2. 2
    Click 'Farmer's Corner' → 'New Farmer Registration'

    On the home page, find the Farmer's Corner section and click New Farmer Registration.

  3. 3
    Enter your Aadhaar number and OTP

    Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and select your state. An OTP will be sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.

  4. 4
    Fill the registration form carefully

    Enter personal details, land details from your Khatoni, and bank account information. Ensure all spellings match your documents exactly.

  5. 5
    Submit and save your reference number

    After submitting, save your registration reference number for tracking your application status.

  6. 6
    Complete e-KYC verification

    Log in to the PM Kisan portal and complete e-KYC using your Aadhaar OTP, or visit a CSC centre. This is now mandatory to receive payments.

Benefits of the Scheme

  • ₹6,000 per year in 3 instalments of ₹2,000 each — directly into your bank account
  • No repayment required — it is financial support, not a loan
  • Money arrives before farming season to buy seeds and fertiliser
  • Available to eligible farmers across all states and union territories

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Name spelling mismatch between application and Aadhaar — even one letter difference causes rejection
  • Wrong bank account number — double-check every digit
  • Bank account not linked to Aadhaar — money won't reach you even if approved
  • Land not in your name in official records
  • Skipping e-KYC — this stops future instalment payments

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I applied months ago but received no money. What should I do?
Check your beneficiary status on pmkisan.gov.in → Farmer's Corner → Beneficiary Status. If showing 'Pending Verification', your state is still reviewing. If 'Rejected', the reason will be mentioned. Call the helpline at 155261 for further help.
Q: Can tenant farmers / sharecroppers apply?
The scheme is primarily for landowners. Some states have provisions for tenant farmers. Check with your local Agriculture Department office.
Q: What is e-KYC and why is it required?
e-KYC verifies that the registered farmer's details are correct and up to date. It can be done online via Aadhaar OTP at the PM Kisan portal or at a CSC centre. It is now mandatory to continue receiving payments.
