Eligibility by Occupation

🌾 If You Are a Farmer

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi, Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, PM Kusum Yojana, Soil Health Card Scheme. Key requirement: Agricultural land ownership and Aadhaar-linked bank account.

💼 If You Run a Small Business

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana, Startup India, Stand Up India, PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana.

👷 If You Are Unemployed or in Daily Wage Work

PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (free training), MGNREGA (100 days paid work for rural households), PM Jan Dhan Yojana.

Common Documents Needed for Most Schemes

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory for almost all central schemes)
  • Bank account linked to Aadhaar (for Direct Benefit Transfer)
  • Income certificate from a government authority
  • Residence proof (voter ID, ration card, utility bill)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Mobile number registered with Aadhaar
