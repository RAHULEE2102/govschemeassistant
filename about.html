About Government Scheme Assistant India

An independent platform dedicated to making government scheme information accessible to every citizen.

⚠️ Legal Notice

This website is NOT affiliated with the Government of India, any state government, any ministry, or any government department. We do not process applications, charge fees, or collect personal data for applications. All information is for educational purposes only.

Who We Are

Government Scheme Assistant India is an independent website created to help ordinary Indian citizens learn about government schemes in a simple and straightforward way. We are a team of public policy researchers, content writers, and digital educators — not connected to any government department or authority.

We believe that lack of awareness is the biggest reason why eligible citizens do not receive government benefits. Many people do not apply simply because they do not understand the process or fear making mistakes.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to make government scheme information accessible to every Indian citizen, regardless of their education level, language skills, or location. We write everything in plain English so that even first-time applicants in rural areas can understand and act on the information.

What We Do

  • Research government schemes from official sources and rewrite them in simple English
  • Create step-by-step application guides for each scheme
  • Prepare documents checklists so applicants know exactly what to bring
  • Explain common mistakes and how to avoid them
  • Provide direct links to official government (.gov.in) websites
  • Update pages regularly to reflect the latest scheme changes

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not apply for schemes on your behalf
  • We do not charge any fees for information
  • We do not collect personal information for applications
  • We do not represent the Government of India or any agency
  • We are not responsible for decisions made by government authorities

Why You Can Trust This Website

All information is sourced from official government portals, press releases, and public policy documents. We clearly link to the official application website for every scheme. This website will never ask for money, promise guaranteed benefits, or make claims on behalf of the government.

If you find any information that is incorrect or outdated, please contact us and we will correct it promptly.
